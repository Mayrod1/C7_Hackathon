Beautifully Magical Monkeys

About the App

This app displays popular tweets, hash tags, and photos on Flickr for a specific area or your location. It performs these actions by searching through the data on Twitter, Flickr, API (application programming interface) and displaying the tweets as markers on the map. You can then click on these markers and either go to the links mentioned in the tweets or view the photos related to the tweets/flickr photos.
